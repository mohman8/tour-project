Hi there, I am Nurudeen
Mechanical Engineer

Learn More

Nurudeen is a final year student studying Mechanical Engineering at Bayero university Kano. He is a young energy leader with dynamic experience in energy innovation and entrepreneurship.

He focuses on improving energy access in Sub-Saharan Africa. He is the CEO of Sirius-X Energy, the fasted growing clean energy startup in Northwest region of Nigeria. He leads a project on solar wind hybrid system capable of providing uninterrupted electricity to households and businesses. Nurudeen is a fellow of Africa fellowship for young energy leaders and a fellow of Students Energy. He was awarded the third best clean energy business idea by Africa Fellowship for young Energy leaders. He was also awarded the most outstanding innovation at Nigeria International Energy Submit 2022.

He hopes to contribute to the Nigeria's technology and energy sector. He is passionate about sustainable energy and energy commodity trading as a young leader positive about the achievement of SDG 7 and 8 respectively.
